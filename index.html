PC Assemble

Through the PC assembly, I not only understand the knowledge of building the PC deeply but also gain the hands-on experience. My team and I practiced assembling and disassembling a PC by working collaboratively. In the process, I recognize key components of the system unit, such as RAM, hard disk driver, power supply unit, motherboard, and CPU. Furthermore, I receive the important note that we should not touch the gold pins or chips because they are sensitive, and handling them directly risks bending pins or damaging the chips themselves.

Furthermore, I suggest providing more system units to ensure that every student has the opportunity for individual hands-on experience. It can ensure that every student understands the assembly workflow clearly and stays engaged in the PC assembly. It can prevent students from becoming mere observers while their teammates do the work and avoid an unfair workload. It enhances the learning experience deeply for each student since they build up the PC by individuals.
